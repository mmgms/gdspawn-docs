Settings and ConfigurationÔÉÅ

In the Project Settings you can configure the GdSpawn settings and shortcuts. You have to turn on Advanced Settings.

  1. Global thumbnail preview preset, can be overridden by libraries and scenes individual presets.

  2. Angles of the global preview thumbnail when the preset is Default.

  3. Rotation increment when pressing Shift.

  4. Enable Tooltips in the 3D editor main window.

Theese are the shourtcuts you can edit.
